# domain-block-list

This is a list of my blocklist.  This list mainly consist of places that I do not frequent nor do I want them tracking me.

I will go through my blocklist line by line to explain my reasoning for it.

## Facebook/Meta

I do not utilize Facebook or any social media that is associated with Facebook, such as Instagram.  Therefore I block all of Facebook/Meta's domains to prevent tracking cross-site.

## Twitter/X

I also block X/Twitter since I don't utilize their service.  They have been known to popup around on the internet.  I just don't want them tracking me.

## .run TLD

I just don't want any confusion when it comes to executing commands.  So I just block the .run

Some other TLDs I was considering blocking

- .zip
- .mov

## URL Shortners 

In general, I do not like the anonymous URL shortners.  I prefer the ones that you can tell where they come from, for example youtu.be is YouTube's URL shortner.  Therefore I block:
- bit.ly
- buff.ly

## Google Login Popup 

I love you Google, but just to read a news article, I don't need to sign-in to my Google Account to read an article.  Therefore I did a block on the sign-in page.
